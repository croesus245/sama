This page helps test and access realtor registration on the MWG Hostels platform.

✅ Current Realtor Registration Access Points

  1. Main Page CTA Button: "Register as Realtor" button in hero section
  2. Quick Access Card: "Register Now" button in List Property section
  3. Realtor Portal: "Register as Realtor" link in realtor-login.html
  4. Direct Modal: JavaScript function openModal('realtorModal')

🏠 Go to Main Page 🏢 Open Realtor Portal

⚠️ Common Issues & Solutions

  • Modal won't open: Check that JavaScript is enabled and modalSystem is loaded
  • Form validation errors: Ensure all required fields are filled properly
  • Password requirements: Must contain uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character
  • Email format: Must be a valid business email address
  • Terms agreement: Must accept realtor terms and conditions

📋 Registration Requirements Checklist

  • ✅ Business Name (minimum 2 characters)
  • ✅ Contact Person Full Name
  • ✅ Business Email Address
  • ✅ Business Phone Number (minimum 10 digits)
  • ✅ Complete Business Address (minimum 10 characters)
  • ✅ Business Type Selection
  • ✅ Years in Business
  • ✅ Strong Password (8+ chars, uppercase, lowercase, number, special char)
  • ✅ Password Confirmation Match
  • ✅ Agreement to Terms & Conditions
  • 🆓 Business Website (Optional)

🎉 After Successful Registration

Once registered successfully, realtors will:

  1. Receive a success message confirmation
  2. Be notified that verification is required (24-48 hours)
  3. Get access to the admin verification panel for review
  4. Receive email notification once verified
  5. Gain access to the realtor dashboard for property management
